Salem Witch Trials

This video gives a brief explanation of what happened during the Salem Witch Trials and how multiple people were accused of Witchcraft without any concrete evidence. Whether innocent or not, people would be forced to confess. It didn't stop until the Governor of Massachusetts's own wife was accused of being a witch, which prompted authorities to stop the trials and release prisoners.

Do you have aphantasia?

This video is about Aphantasia, the inability to clearly visualize images in the mind’s eye. 4% of the world population have it. Mental imagery exist on a spectrum, on one end there’s people with Aphantasia and at the other end people with Hyperpantasia with very vivid mental imagery, most people fall somewhere in the middle of these two.
